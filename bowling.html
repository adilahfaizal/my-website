MY BOWLING CAREER

I have been bowling since I was 5 years old, influenced by my dad, who loved to play bowling and worked as a bowling technician. In 2012, my dad opened his own bowling center in Bukit Beruntung, which inspired me to pursue a career in bowling. Since then, I joined the Seintak Bowling Club (Perangsang Selangor) for training and began participating in various tournaments, including the MSSD, MSSS, and MSSM.

When I was 10 years old, I competed in my first league tournament, the National Junior Circuit, which involved participants from all states in Malaysia.

In 2019, when I was 15 years old, I achieved a top-3 finish in the MSSM tournaments, winning a silver medal in both the All Events and the Single Event. Unfortunately, I couldn't complete my bowling career during high school due to COVID-19. Fortunately, I was able to revive my bowling career by participating in events at my university, such as Mini Karisma.
