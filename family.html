MY LOVELY FAMILY

Daddy

This is my dad. His name is Mohd Faizal and everyone calls him "Jai." He was born in 1976 in Taiping, Perak, and he is truly the best dad I could ever ask for. Dad is incredibly skilled in many things he is good in business, electrical work, cooking, and even cutting hair. No matter how busy he is with his own work, he always makes time for me. He never hesitates to help with anything I need and always gives me what I want. For example, he consistently picks me up and drops me off whenever I am going to work, showing his love and support without question. His kindness and selflessness mean the world to me. I hope that one day, I will marry a man who can do everything just like my dad.

Mummy

This is my mom Rinieta Aini and everyone calls her "Ieta." She was born in 1976 in Cheras, Selangor. One of her greatest passions is hiking, and almost every weekend she spends her time on the trails, exploring nature with my aunt. She is not just an incredible mom, but also my biggest supporter which always encouraging me in both my bowling journey and my studies. Her strength and determination are unmatched, and she has taught me the importance of perseverance, reminding me to never give up even when faced with challenges. I really admire my mom how she handles every situation with grace and resilience. I hope to grow up to be as strong and confident as she is, especially when life gets tough. She is truly my role model, and I am so grateful to have her in my life.

Brother

This is my brother. Muhammad Fariezul Fariz, also known as ‘Fariz’ or ‘Ayish’. He is 23 years old and currently pursuing a Degree in Finance at the International Islamic University Malaysia. He is truly a gentleman and always shows great care for me. Whenever we hang out, he kindly carries my handbag and treats me to whatever I want. His thoughtfulness and generosity never fail to make me feel special.
